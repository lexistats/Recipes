4 Essential Cooking Skills

Master these fundamentals before starting.

  1. Read the Recipe
    • Understand the flow
    • Know the timing
    • Have the necessary ingredients ready
  2. Preparation
    • Make sure kitchen is clean
    • Measure ingredients
    • Chop vegetables
  3. Sanitation
    • Wash hands for 20 seconds
    • Sanitize countertops
    • Use separate cutting boards for raw meats and produce
  4. Get Creative!
    • Pick your favorite seasonings
    • Season as you go
    • Add liquids to keep meats tender and juicy
