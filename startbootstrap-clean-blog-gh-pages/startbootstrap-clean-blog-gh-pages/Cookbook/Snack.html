Betty Crocker Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies

Snack Cookies/Bars
Serves: 48 Prep: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 package (12 oz) semisweet chocolate chips (2 cups)
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped nuts, if desired

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. In large bowl, beat granulated sugar, brown sugar, butter, vanilla and egg with electric mixer on medium speed, or mix with spoon, until well blended.
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt (dough will be stiff). Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  4. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Cool 1 to 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet. Cool on wire rack.

Cake Batter Rice Krispie Treats

Snack
Serves: 12 Prep: 5 mins Cook: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 5 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1½ cups funfetti frosting
  • 6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
  • ¼ cup funfetti or rainbow sprinkles (optional topping)

Directions

  1. Spray a 9×13 inch pan with nonstick cooking spray and set aside.
  2. In a stock pot, melt butter, mini marshmallows and vanilla until just melted. Be careful not to overcook.
  3. Remove from heat once melted and add the frosting.
  4. Stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pan and press it in with your hands or a rubber spatula.
  6. Sprinkle extra rainbow or funfetti sprinkles on top.
  7. Let cool for 20 minutes.
  8. Slice into squares and enjoy.

Cake Mix Grasshopper Sandwich Cookies

Snack Cookies/Bars
Serves: 15 Prep: 5 mins Cook: 8 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 oz) box Devils Food cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup vegetable shortening
  • ½ cup butter, softened to room temp
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons peppermint extract
  • 3 drops green food coloring

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix together cake mix, eggs and shortening until well combined. The dough will be pretty thick.
  3. Roll dough into 1-inch balls, making sure they are all similar in size.
  4. Place dough onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 8 minutes.
  5. Immediately transfer cookies to a cooling rack.
  6. To make the frosting, mix together butter, milk, powdered sugar, peppermint extract and green food coloring until smooth.
  7. Spread frosting onto cooled cookies and sandwich two together.
