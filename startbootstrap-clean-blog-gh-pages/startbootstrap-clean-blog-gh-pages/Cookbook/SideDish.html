Roasted Air Fryer Carrots (Sweet or Savory)

Side Dish Vegetable
Serves: 4 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • Sweet:
  • 1 lb carrots, washed, peeled, and chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Savory:
  • 1 lb medium carrots, washed and peeled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped parsley (optional)

Directions

  1. Sweet Carrots: Pour melted butter, brown sugar, and salt over carrots and stir until fully coated. Place in an oven-safe bowl that fits your air fryer. Cook at 380°F for 20–25 minutes, stirring halfway.
  2. Savory Carrots: Toss carrots in olive oil, garlic powder, and paprika. Place in the air fryer basket and cook at 380°F for 20 minutes, shaking halfway through. Once done, top with parmesan cheese and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Roasted Cheesy Vegetables

Side Dish Vegetable
Cook: 30 mins Temp: 425°F

Ingredients

  • 1 lb vegetable medley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon fresh ground pepper
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1½ tablespoons grated cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Mix together the olive oil, salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Toss the vegetable medley in the spice mix and spread out on the baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 30 minutes, stirring once in the middle.
  5. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese and serve immediately.

Roasted Parmesan Potatoes

Side Dish Potatoes Vegetable
Serves: 6 Prep: 5 mins Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 6 russet potatoes, skin on, scrubbed and diced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1½ teaspoons paprika
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with nonstick cooking spray.
  3. Spread diced potatoes on the foil-lined pan.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the potatoes and toss with your hands until fully coated.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle over the potatoes and toss again to coat.
  6. Bake for 35–45 minutes, stirring every 10–15 minutes so they cook evenly and don't burn on the bottom.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with additional salt and pepper if desired.
