Big Mac Sliders

Appetizer Main Dish Beef Sandwich
Serves: 6 Prep: 20 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 12 oz King Hawaiian rolls (1 package)
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp French dressing
  • 4 tsp sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tbsp minced onion
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • ½ onion, diced
  • ½ tsp seasoned salt
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 6 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sliced dill pickles
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • Melted butter + sesame seeds for topping

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray a 9×13 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Slice rolls horizontally (keeping them connected) and place in the bottom of the prepared dish.
  3. Whisk together mayonnaise, French dressing, pickle relish, onion, vinegar, sugar, and salt to make the special sauce. Set aside.
  4. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef, onion, seasoned salt, and pepper until onions are translucent and beef is browned. Drain excess grease.
  5. Spread half the special sauce on the bottom rolls.
  6. Spread half the beef mixture on top, then layer on the cheese slices.
  7. Add remaining beef mixture, then spread pickles and lettuce evenly on top.
  8. Spread remaining special sauce on the inside of the top rolls and place on top.
  9. Brush melted butter on top and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Cut into individual sliders and serve.

Cheese Ball

Appetizer
Quick & Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 oz pkg) cream cheese
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar (optional)
  • 1 tsp celery salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dry mustard
  • Chopped walnuts (for rolling)

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together until well combined.
  2. Shape into a ball and roll in chopped walnuts.
  3. Refrigerate until firm. Serve with crackers.

Crockpot Cheeseburger Queso Dip

Appetizer
Cook: 1.5–2 hours (slow cooker)

Ingredients

  • ½ lb lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup yellow onion, diced
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (16 oz) package Velveeta, cubed
  • 1 (10 oz) can Rotel, undrained
  • 3–4 green onions, sliced
  • Chips, crackers, or fresh veggies for serving

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain grease. Add onion, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic. Cook until onions are translucent.
  2. Add beef mixture, Velveeta cubes, and Rotel to a small slow cooker. Cover and cook on high for 1½–2 hours, stirring every 30 minutes until cheese is melted.
  3. Stir thoroughly, garnish with green onions, and serve immediately with chips, crackers, or veggies.

Skinny Poolside Dip

Appetizer Snack
Serves: 6 Prep: 2 mins Cook: 3 mins

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and finely diced
  • 1 small can black olives, finely diced
  • 15 oz canned corn, drained
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning (2 tbsp)

Directions

  1. Dump everything into a bowl and stir together. As the vegetables release their water, the mixture will come together more easily. Continue stirring until well combined.
  2. Serve immediately or store in the fridge, covered, for up to 24 hours.
  3. Serve with crackers or chips.

Notes

The longer the dip sets, the more water the vegetables will release — best eaten sooner than later. Double up the jalapeño or leave in the seeds for more heat.
