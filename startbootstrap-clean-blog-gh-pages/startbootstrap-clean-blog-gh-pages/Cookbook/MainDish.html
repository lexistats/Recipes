20-Minute 7-Layer Chicken Tacos

Main Dish Chicken
Serves: 6 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded (~1–1½ lbs)
  • 1 (4.5 oz) can green chiles, drained
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ⅓ cup sour cream (+ more for topping)
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 10 hard taco shells
  • ¾ cup refried beans
  • ½ cup shredded Mexican or Feta cheese
  • ⅓ cup cilantro, chopped
  • 20 cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine shredded chicken, green chiles, honey, ⅓ cup sour cream, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Place taco shells in a 9×13-inch pan. Spread ~1 tbsp refried beans on the bottom of each shell.
  4. Add ~¼ cup chicken mixture on top of beans. Sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Bake 10 minutes until cheese is melted and chicken is warm.
  6. Top with sour cream, cilantro, tomatoes, and avocado. Serve immediately.

Notes

Also delicious with a little ranch or salsa on top.

Air Fryer Loaded Mini Meatloaf

Main Dish Beef
Serves: 4 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 12 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 3 oz chili sauce
  • 2 slices thick-cut bacon, cooked and chopped
  • ¾ cup sharp cheddar, shredded
  • ¼ cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp seasoned salt
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp yellow mustard
  • ¼ cup BBQ sauce (for topping)

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except BBQ sauce in a mixing bowl. Gently mix — don't overwork. Add more Panko if mixture is too loose.
  2. Form into 4–6 mini loaves. Place in air fryer basket.
  3. Make a small indentation in the top of each loaf. Spoon BBQ sauce into the indentation and spread lightly over the top.
  4. Air fry at 400°F for 10–12 minutes until nicely browned.

Baked Chicken Bacon Ranch Taquitos

Main Dish Chicken
Serves: 20 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 12 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 3 tbsp green onions, chopped
  • 1 (1 oz) packet dry Ranch dressing mix, divided
  • 20 (6-inch) flour tortillas
  • Salt, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Cover a large baking sheet with foil and spray lightly with cooking spray.
  2. Mix together cream cheese, bacon, chicken, cheese, and green onions. Add about half the Ranch mix; stir well.
  3. Spoon 2–3 tbsp of filling onto each tortilla; roll up and place seam-side down on baking sheet.
  4. Spray tops with cooking spray; sprinkle with salt.
  5. Bake 15–20 minutes until edges and tops are golden.
  6. Serve with ranch dressing or salsa.

Baked Chicken Flautas

Main Dish Chicken
Serves: 8 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie works great)
  • 1 cup salsa verde
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (15.25 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15 oz) can corn, drained
  • 1½ cups Colby Jack cheese, shredded, divided
  • 12–14 flour tortillas (soft taco size)

Directions

Baked Cream Cheese Spaghetti Casserole

Main Dish Beef Casserole Pasta
Serves: 8 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1 (26 oz) jar prepared spaghetti sauce
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, room temperature
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Brown ground beef; drain fat. Stir in spaghetti sauce. Set aside.
  3. Cook spaghetti; drain. Toss with cream cheese, Italian seasoning, and garlic until cream cheese is melted and spaghetti is coated.
  4. Spread a little meat sauce in a greased 9×13" pan. Add spaghetti on top, then remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  5. Bake 30 minutes until bubbly.

Notes

Freeze half: cool completely before adding to a freezer container. Top with cheese. Freeze up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in fridge, then bake as directed.

Baked Honey Lime Chicken Tenders

Main Dish Chicken
Serves: 4 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (or tenders)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • ¾ cup flour
  • ½ cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Sauce: 1½ tbsp lime juice, ½ cup honey, 1 tbsp hot sauce, ½ cup brown sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Slice chicken into 2-inch strips.
  2. Combine salt, pepper, flour, and breadcrumbs in a large zip bag. Add chicken and shake to coat.
  3. Melt butter in a 9×13 pan in the oven. Add chicken evenly spaced.
  4. Bake 12 minutes. Flip chicken; bake 10 more minutes.
  5. Whisk together all sauce ingredients. Pour over chicken and coat all pieces.
  6. Bake 5 more minutes. Serve.

Beef Enchiladas

Main Dish Beef
Serves: 8 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 batch red enchilada sauce (or 15 oz can)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small white onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1½ lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 (15 oz) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (4 oz) can diced green chiles
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • 3 cups Mexican-blend shredded cheese
  • Toppings: fresh cilantro, chopped red onions, diced avocado, sour cream, crumbled cotija cheese

Directions

  1. Prepare enchilada sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a large sauté pan over medium-high. Sauté onion and garlic 3 minutes. Add ground beef and cumin; cook until browned, crumbling as it cooks. Stir in beans and green chiles. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oven to 350°F. Spread ½ cup enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 9×13" baking dish.
  4. Lay out a tortilla and spread 2 tbsp sauce over the surface. Add a generous spoonful of beef filling down the center, then sprinkle with ⅓ cup cheese. Roll up and place seam-side down in the dish. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
  5. Spread any remaining sauce over the top of the enchiladas, followed by any extra cheese.
  6. Bake uncovered 20 minutes until enchiladas are cooked through and tortillas are slightly crispy on the outside. Transfer to a wire rack.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with cilantro, red onion, avocado, sour cream, and/or cotija cheese.

Cheeseburger Soup

Main Dish Soup
Serves: 8 (2¼ qts) Prep: 45 mins Cook: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • ½ lb ground beef
  • 4 tbsp butter, divided
  • ¾ cup chopped onion
  • ¾ cup shredded carrots
  • ¾ cup diced celery
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried parsley flakes
  • 1¾ lbs (~4 cups) cubed peeled potatoes
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2–4 cups shredded Velveeta
  • 1½ cups whole milk
  • ¾ tsp salt, ¼–½ tsp pepper
  • ¼ cup sour cream

Directions

  1. Cook and crumble beef in a large saucepan until no longer pink; drain and set aside.
  2. Melt 1 tbsp butter in the same pan. Sauté onion, carrots, celery, basil, and parsley ~10 minutes until tender. Add potatoes, beef, and broth; bring to a boil. Simmer covered until potatoes are tender, 10–12 minutes.
  3. In a small skillet, melt remaining 3 tbsp butter. Add flour and cook, stirring, 3–5 minutes until bubbly. Add to soup; bring to a boil and stir 2 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to low. Stir in cheese, milk, salt, and pepper; cook until cheese melts. Remove from heat; blend in sour cream.

Cheesy Ground Beef and Rice Casserole

Main Dish Beef Casserole
Serves: 6 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (85% lean)
  • 1½ tsp Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
  • 3 tbsp butter, separated
  • ¾ cup diced onions
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2½ cups beef broth
  • 1¼ cups uncooked long-grain white rice
  • 8 oz sliced button mushrooms
  • 10.5 oz cream of mushroom soup
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar, divided

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Brown beef in a large pot with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Drain; set aside covered.
  2. Melt 2 tbsp butter in the same pot. Cook onions 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 1 more minute.
  3. Add beef broth, remaining butter, and rice. Stir; bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer. Cover tightly and cook 10 minutes.
  4. Add mushrooms, replace cover, and cook 5–10 more minutes. Turn heat off; let stand 10 minutes without stirring.
  5. Stir in beef, cream of mushroom soup, milk, sour cream, and half the cheese. Transfer to a greased 9×13 casserole dish; top with remaining cheese.
  6. Cover and bake 20 minutes. Serve.

Chicken Alfredo Baked Ziti

Main Dish Chicken Casserole
Serves: 6–8 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 12 oz uncooked ziti
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1½ cups shredded part-skim mozzarella
  • Fresh Parmesan + basil or parsley to top
  • Alfredo Sauce:
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 cup low-fat milk
  • ¾ cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp black pepper

Directions

  1. Alfredo Sauce: Heat oil in a saucepan over medium-high. Sauté garlic 1 minute. Sprinkle with flour; cook 1 more minute. Slowly whisk in stock until smooth. Whisk in milk; simmer until thickened. Stir in Parmesan, salt, and pepper until melted.

  3. Cook pasta al dente; drain. Toss with chicken and alfredo sauce.
  4. Pour half into baking dish; sprinkle with 1 cup cheese. Layer remaining pasta; top with remaining ½ cup cheese.
  5. Bake 20–25 minutes until cheese is melted and just starting to turn golden. Serve immediately.

Chicken Enchiladas

Main Dish Chicken
Serves: 8 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp avocado or olive oil
  • 1 small white onion, diced
  • 1½ lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, diced into ½-inch pieces
  • 1 (4 oz) can diced green chiles
  • Sea salt and freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained

Chicken Parmesan Casserole

Main Dish Chicken Pasta
Serves: 5 Prep: 40 mins Cook: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • ¾ lb rigatoni pasta
  • 32 oz marinara sauce
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella, divided
  • ⅓ cup finely grated Parmesan
  • 2 small boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • ¾ cup flour + 2 tsp seasoned salt + ¼ tsp pepper
  • 2 eggs, whisked
  • 1½ cups Italian breadcrumbs
  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Fresh parsley to garnish

Directions

  1. Cook rigatoni 1 minute less than al dente; drain. Toss with marinara, ⅓ Parmesan, and 1 cup mozzarella. Set aside.
  2. Pat chicken dry; cut into ½-inch strips. Dredge in flour mixture → eggs → breadcrumbs. Flatten gently with palms.
  3. Heat ¼ inch of oil + 2 tbsp butter in a pan over medium-high. Fry chicken strips ~4 minutes per side until golden. Drain on paper towels. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 9×13-inch dish. Add half the pasta, then half the chicken, then ⅓ Parmesan and 1 cup mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake uncovered 25 minutes. For a browner top, increase to 425°F for 5 more minutes. Garnish with parsley.

Shortcut

Frozen chicken tenders work great — let sit 5 minutes at room temperature, slice into strips, and add directly. No changes to bake time needed.

Chicken Salad with Grapes

Main Dish Salad Sandwich
Yield: 4 cups Prep: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups chopped or shredded chicken (canned works great)
  • ½ cup diced celery
  • ½ cup red seedless grapes, halved or quartered
  • ¼ cup sliced almonds (or chopped pecans)
  • ¼ cup light mayo
  • ¼ cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp dried minced onion
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey
  • ¼ tsp celery salt

Directions

  1. Toast almonds in a nonstick pan over medium heat, stirring until fragrant and lightly golden.
  2. Stir together mayo, Greek yogurt, honey, dried onion, celery salt, and apple cider vinegar. Add chicken, celery, grapes, and almonds. Mix well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate at least 25 minutes before serving to let flavors meld. Serve with lettuce, bread, crackers, chips, or celery.

Classic Homestyle Chicken Noodle Soup

Main Dish Soup
Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 11 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pkg (24 oz) Reames Homestyle Egg Noodles
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 2 cups chopped celery
  • 8 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 cups heavy cream or half-and-half
  • ½ tsp thyme, 2 tsp parsley, 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 lb precooked boneless skinless chicken
  • ½ cup sherry (optional)

Directions

  1. Bring chicken broth to a boil with bay leaves.

  • 1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese, cubed and softened

Delicious Ham and Potato Soup

Main DishSoup
Serves: 8Prep: 20 minsCook: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 3½ cups peeled and diced potatoes
  • ⅓ cup diced celery (or ½ tsp celery seed)
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped onion
  • ¾ cup diced cooked ham
  • 3¼ cups water
  • 2 tbsp chicken bouillon granules
  • ½ tsp minced garlic, salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 5 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk

Directions

  1. Combine potatoes, celery, onion, ham, and water in a stockpot. Boil, then cook over medium heat until potatoes are tender, 10–15 minutes. Stir in bouillon, salt, and pepper.

Easy 20-Minute Skillet Lasagna

Main DishBeefPasta
Serves: 8Prep: 8 minsCook: 12 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 12 oz bow tie pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 26 oz spaghetti sauce
  • 1 tsp salt, ½ tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella, divided
  • ½ cup sour cream

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet; set aside.
  2. Cook pasta per package directions; drain and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce to pasta, then beef, seasonings, ½ cup cheese, and sour cream.
  4. Mix and heat over low for 5 minutes until cheese melts. Sprinkle with remaining cheese if desired.

Easy Lasagna

Main DishBeefCasserolePasta
Serves: 15Prep: 20 minsCook: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ½ lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 48 oz spaghetti sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tsp dried parsley, ¼ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles (uncooked)
  • ¼ cup water

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Brown beef and sausage; drain. Add garlic and sauce; simmer 5 minutes.
  2. Mix ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella, eggs, ½ cup Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Spread ¾ cup sauce in a greased 9×13 pan. Layer: 3 uncooked noodles → ⅓ cheese mixture → ¼ sauce. Repeat twice. Top with 3 noodles, remaining sauce, 1 cup mozzarella, and remaining Parmesan.
  4. Add ¼–½ cup water to edges if sauce is thick. Cover with sprayed foil (spray side down). Bake 50 min. Uncover; bake 10 more min. Let stand 10 min before cutting.

Notes

Don't spray the pan — the bottom noodle can burn. Try without cooking spray.

Easy Orange Chicken (No Deep Frying)

Main DishChicken
Serves: 2Prep: 15 minsCook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp green onion, chopped (optional garnish)
  • Sauce: 1 cup fresh orange juice · 2 tbsp white vinegar · ¼ cup + 2 tbsp brown sugar · 2 tbsp soy sauce · 2 tsp minced garlic · ¼ tsp ginger powder · 1 tbsp cornstarch

Directions

  1. Mix all sauce ingredients; set aside.
  2. Toss chicken pieces in cornstarch until evenly coated (chicken should be moist — do not pat dry).
  3. Heat oil in a wok or pan over medium-high until bubbling around a chopstick. Add chicken; spread apart and do not move. Sear 5–6 minutes per side until golden crispy brown (~10–12 min total). Transfer to a plate; pour out excess oil.
  4. In the same pan over medium-high, pour in sauce. Stir constantly until it bubbles and thickens, ~2 minutes. Add chicken; toss to coat. Garnish with green onions.

Air Fryer Option

Air fry cornstarch-coated chicken at 400°F for 12–14 minutes until crispy. Then toss with sauce as directed.

Easy Sloppy Joes

Main DishBeefSandwich
Serves: 6Prep: 5 minsCook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can (10¾ oz) Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
  • 2 tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp garlic powder

Directions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet over medium-high until well browned, stirring to break apart. Drain fat.
  2. Stir in soup, brown sugar, Worcestershire, lemon juice, mustard, and garlic powder. Cook 5 minutes.
  3. Season to taste. Serve on buns.

Gourmet Chicken Pizza

Main DishChicken
Yield: 1 pizzaPrep: 15 minsCook: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (or 12.5 oz canned chicken)
  • 1 (10 oz) can refrigerated pizza crust
  • ½ cup Ranch-style or lite Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar (or 2 cups pizza cheese)
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Grease a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  2. Cook chicken in a skillet over medium-high until no longer pink. Cool, then shred or chop.
  3. Press dough into pan. Bake 4–5 minutes until beginning to turn golden.
  4. Spread ranch dressing over crust. Sprinkle mozzarella. Add tomatoes, green onions, and chicken on top; finish with cheddar.
  5. Return to oven 7–10 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Hamburger Casserole

Main DishBeefCasserole
Serves: 12Prep: 10 minsCook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb box medium shell pasta
  • 1 lb ground beef, browned with garlic powder, dried onion, salt, and pepper (1 tsp each)
  • 2 (10.75 oz) cans tomato soup + ½ can water
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella

Directions

  1. Cook noodles per package; drain.
  2. Add tomato soup and ½ can water to browned beef. Stir and cook over medium 3 minutes.
  3. Pour noodles into a greased 9×13 dish. Pour beef mixture over and stir. Sprinkle with cheese; cover with foil.
  4. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes. Remove foil; bake 10 more minutes. Broil 3–5 minutes for browned cheese if desired.

Herb Roasted Turkey

Main DishTurkey
Temp: 350°F · See times below

Ingredients

  • 8–24 lb turkey, fresh or thawed
  • 1 tbsp flour (for oven bag)
  • 2 stalks celery, 1 medium onion
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 tbsp dried sage
  • 1 tsp each: dried thyme, basil, rosemary, paprika, seasoned salt
  • ½ tsp pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Shake flour in a Reynolds Oven Bag (turkey size); place in a large roasting pan at least 2 inches deep.
  2. Add vegetables to bag. Remove neck and giblets from turkey; rinse, pat dry, and brush with oil.
  3. Combine seasonings; sprinkle and rub evenly over turkey. Place turkey in bag on top of vegetables.
  4. Close bag with nylon tie; cut six ½-inch slits in top. Insert meat thermometer through slit into thickest part of inner thigh (not touching bone).
  5. Bake until thermometer reads 180°F: 8–12 lbs: 1½–2 hrs · 12–16 lbs: 2–2½ hrs · 16–20 lbs: 2½–3 hrs · 20–24 lbs: 3–3½ hrs.
  6. Let stand in bag 15 minutes. Cut open top; use two carving forks to lift turkey out.

Honey Lime Chicken Enchiladas

Main DishChickenCasserole
Serves: 8Prep: 45 minsCook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Marinade: 6 tbsp honey · 5 tbsp lime juice · 1 tbsp chili powder · ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 (16 oz) can green enchilada sauce, divided
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar, divided
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Directions

  1. Whisk marinade and toss with shredded chicken. Marinate in fridge at least 30 minutes (a few hours is better).
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F. Pour ½ cup enchilada sauce in the bottom of a 9×13 pan.
  3. Fill tortillas with chicken and ~1 cup cheese (reserve 1 cup for topping). Roll and place in pan.
  4. Mix remaining enchilada sauce, heavy cream, and any leftover marinade; pour over enchiladas. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake 30 minutes until brown and crispy on top.

Notes

1 cup of chicken ≈ 5¼ oz.

Pulled Pork

Main DishPork
Serves: 6Prep: 20 minsCook: 5 hrs (oven) / 8 hrs (slow cooker)

Ingredients

  • 4 lb pork shoulder or butt
  • 2 tbsp oil (for searing, optional)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp each onion powder, garlic powder, cumin, kosher salt, black pepper
  • 12 oz Coke (not diet)
  • BBQ sauce for coating (optional)

Directions

  1. Trim pork and cut into 4 pieces. Rub spices all over (can be done the night before).
  2. Optional sear: heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high; sear all sides a few seconds each.
  3. Oven: Preheat to 300°F. Pour coke around pork; cover and cook 3 hrs. Remove lid; cook 1–2 more hrs until tender. Shred and toss with BBQ sauce.
  4. Slow Cooker: Place pork in slow cooker; pour coke around it. Cook on LOW 8 hrs or high 4–5 hrs until tender and easily shreds.
  5. Instant Pot: Place pork and coke in Instant Pot. Cook on Manual/High pressure 70 minutes. Natural release ~15 minutes. Shred.

Instant Pot Beef Stew

Main DishBeefSoup
Serves: 6Prep: 10 minsCook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef stew meat (or roast cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 lb baby carrots (halved)
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp salt, ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1 oz dry onion soup mix (optional)

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in the Instant Pot; stir briefly.

Instant Pot Cream Cheese Chili

Main DishSoupChicken
Serves: 6Prep: 10 minsCook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15 oz) can corn, undrained
  • 1 (10 oz) can Rotel, undrained
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 oz packet dry ranch seasoning
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese

Directions

  1. Place chicken in Instant Pot. Combine beans, corn, tomatoes, Worcestershire, ranch, spices, and water; pour over chicken.

Instant Pot Ground Beef and Vegetable Stew

Main DishBeefSoup
Serves: 6Prep: 10 minsCook: 18 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced; 2 celery stalks, sliced; salt and pepper
  • 4 large carrots, thickly sliced
  • 5 potatoes, cut into large cubes
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 8 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 14 oz canned diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 10 oz canned cream of mushroom soup
  • 10 oz frozen mixed vegetables

Directions

  1. SAUTÉ beef, onion, and celery until beef is no longer pink. Season; CANCEL.
  2. Add potatoes, carrots, and onion soup mix. Pour broth, tomato sauce, and diced tomatoes on top; gently mix.
  3. Seal lid. MANUAL 8 minutes. Natural release 15 minutes; quick-release remainder.
  4. Stir in cream of mushroom soup and frozen vegetables. Let sit 3–5 minutes to warm through. Serve.

Instant Pot Honey Sesame Chicken

Main DishChicken
Serves: 6Prep: 10 minsCook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1½ lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup honey
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp oil, 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch dissolved in 3 tbsp water
  • Sesame seeds & sliced green onions for topping

Directions

  1. Place chicken in Instant Pot. Whisk honey, soy sauce, onion, ketchup, oil, garlic, and pepper flakes; pour over chicken.
  2. Seal lid. MANUAL 25 min (frozen) or 18 min (thawed). Quick release.
  3. Remove chicken. Pour cornstarch mixture into pot; SAUTÉ 3–5 minutes until thickened.
  4. Shred chicken; return to pot and toss with sauce. Serve over rice topped with sesame seeds and green onions.

Instant Pot Korean Beef and Rice

Main DishBeef
Serves: 4Prep: 10 minsCook: 12 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced; 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ cup brown sugar, ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger, salt and pepper
  • ½ tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • ½ cup beef broth
  • Rice (cooked in pot): 1½ cups white rice · 1¾ cups water · 1 tbsp butter · ½ tsp salt
  • 5 green onions, diced; sesame seeds (optional)

Directions

  1. SAUTÉ sesame oil. Add beef and garlic; cook ~5 minutes, mostly browned.
  2. Add brown sugar, soy sauce, ginger, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Scrape the bottom. Pour beef broth on top; don't mix. CANCEL SAUTÉ.
  3. Place trivet in pot. Add rinsed rice, water, butter, and salt to a 7-inch oven-safe pan; set on trivet.
  4. Seal lid. MANUAL 7 minutes. Natural release 8 minutes. Remove lid. Remove rice pan; fluff rice.
  5. Stir beef; serve over rice with green onions and sesame seeds.

Instant Pot Orange Chicken

Main DishChicken
Quick & easy pressure cooker dinner

Ingredients

  • 3–4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (~2 lbs), cubed
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced; ½ tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch + 3–4 tbsp water (to thicken)
  • Green onions for garnish

Directions

  1. Mix water, OJ, soy sauce, ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger. Place chicken in Instant Pot; add sauce and stir.
  2. Seal lid. Pressure cook 5 minutes. Release pressure.
  3. Press SAUTÉ. Mix cornstarch and water; add to pot. Cook 1–2 minutes until sauce thickens. Serve garnished with green onions.

Instant Pot Taco Soup

Main DishSoup
Quick pressure cooker soup

Ingredients

  • 1–2 lbs ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (~3 tbsp)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder, ½ tsp onion powder, ½ tsp oregano, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 packet Ranch dressing mix (optional)
  • 1 (10 oz) can Rotel
  • 1 (7 oz) can diced green chiles with juice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can each black beans, garbanzo beans, red kidney or pinto beans (rinsed)
  • 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes with juice
  • 1 cup frozen corn (or 1 drained can)
  • 4–5 corn tortillas cut into strips (optional)

Directions

  1. SAUTÉ meat 3 minutes. Add taco seasoning and remaining spices; cook until browned.
  2. Add Ranch packet, Rotel, green chiles, and broth; stir. Bring to a simmer; add beans. Add diced tomatoes on top (don't stir).
  3. Lock lid; turn to SEALING. Cancel SAUTÉ; press MANUAL 6 minutes.
  4. Natural release 15 minutes; quick-release remaining pressure.
  5. Add tortilla strips (if using) and corn. Stir; let sit a few minutes. Garnish and serve.

Instant Pot Zuppa Toscana

Main DishSoup
Serves: 6Prep: 5 minsCook: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 lb ground mild Italian sausage
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 large russet potatoes, unpeeled, sliced ¼-inch thick
  • 6 cups (1½ qt) chicken broth
  • 2 cups fresh kale, chopped
  • ¾ cup heavy cream, half-and-half, or milk

Directions

  1. SAUTÉ olive oil. Cook onion until translucent. Add sausage; cook until browned. Add garlic; cook 1 minute. Drain excess grease if desired.
  2. Add potatoes and chicken broth. Lock lid; SEAL. Cook on HIGH pressure 5 minutes.
  3. Natural release 10 minutes, then quick release.
  4. Remove lid; stir in kale (the heat will wilt it). Pour in cream; stir to combine.

Korean Beef and Rice

Main DishBeef
Serves: 4Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½–1 tsp crushed red peppers
  • Cooked rice, 1 bunch green onions (diced)

Directions

  1. Brown beef with garlic in sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
