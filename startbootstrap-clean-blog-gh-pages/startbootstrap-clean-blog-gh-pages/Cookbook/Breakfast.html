Breakfast Quiche

Breakfast Egg
Serves: 6 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Crust:
  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup cold butter
  • 3–5 tbsp ice water
  • Filling:
  • 12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
  • ½ cup shredded pepper jack or Monterey Jack
  • ½ cup shredded sharp cheddar
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped onion
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • ⅛ tsp cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Combine flour and salt; cut in butter until crumbly. Add ice water gradually, tossing with a fork until dough holds together. Wrap and refrigerate 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 450°F. Roll dough to ⅛-inch thick; fit into a 9-inch pie plate. Trim and flute edge. Line with double-thickness foil. Bake 5 minutes; remove foil and bake 5 more minutes. Cool. Reduce heat to 375°F.
  3. Sprinkle bacon, cheeses, and onion over crust.
  4. Beat remaining filling ingredients together; pour over top.
  5. Bake 30–35 minutes until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.

Buttermilk Syrup

Breakfast
Quick stovetop

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter (2 sticks), cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp dark corn syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Directions

  1. Combine sugar, butter, buttermilk, and corn syrup in a large heavy pan. Stir until mixture comes to a boil. Cook 3–4 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in baking soda and vanilla until completely dissolved. (Use a large pan — it can boil over!)
  3. Refrigerate leftovers and reheat in the microwave.

Buttermilk Substitute

Mix 1 tbsp lemon juice or white vinegar with 1 cup milk. Stir and let sit 5 minutes.

Easy Baked French Toast

Breakfast Bread
Serves: 4–6 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb French bread baguette, cut into 1-inch slices
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp powdered sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Maple syrup for serving
  • Sliced strawberries for topping (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Arrange bread slices face-up in a greased skillet or baking dish, packed tightly with no overlap.
  3. Whisk together egg, milk, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt. Pour evenly over bread.
  4. Let sit 3–4 minutes to soak in (or refrigerate overnight covered with foil). Flip and soak a few more minutes.
  5. Bake 40–45 minutes until tops are just crispy. Top with powdered sugar and serve with maple syrup.

French Crepe Recipe

Breakfast Brunch Breads & Rolls
Serves: 8 crepes Prep: 10 mins Cook: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cup white wheat flour
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 tsp white sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, eggs, salt, vanilla, melted butter, and sugar.
  2. Gradually beat in milk with a whisk or electric mixer until smooth with no lumps.
  3. Heat a lightly buttered crepe pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour batter onto pan; tip and rotate to spread as thinly as possible.
  5. Cook 1–2 minutes per side until lightly browned. Serve hot with your favorite fillings.

German Pancake with Buttermilk Sauce

Breakfast
Yield: 1 (9×13) pan Prep: 20 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • Buttermilk Sauce:
  • 1½ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 tbsp light corn syrup
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Place butter in a 9×13 pan and melt in oven (don't let it burn).
  2. Blend eggs, milk, salt, and flour until smooth. Pour into prepared pan.
  3. Bake 18 minutes or until golden.
  4. While pancake bakes, combine sugar, buttermilk, butter, corn syrup, and baking soda in a saucepan. Boil 7 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
  5. Spoon sauce over slices of pancake to serve.

Ham and Swiss Quiche

Breakfast Egg
Serves: 8 Prep: 20 mins Cook: 45–50 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie shell
  • 1 cup (4 oz) shredded Swiss cheese, divided
  • 1 cup finely chopped cooked ham
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 can (12 fl oz) Nestlé Carnation Evaporated Milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Sprinkle ½ cup cheese, ham, and green onions into pie shell.
  3. Whisk together evaporated milk, eggs, flour, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Pour mixture into pie shell; sprinkle with remaining ½ cup cheese.
  4. Bake 45–50 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack 10 minutes before serving.

Pancakes

Breakfast
Serves: 8–12 pancakes Prep: 5 mins Cook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1½ cups (375ml) milk
  • 3 eggs

Directions

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and caster sugar into a bowl.
  2. Break eggs into a separate bowl and whisk together with the milk.
  3. Gradually add the milk and egg mixture to the flour mixture, whisking to a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and melt a small knob of butter. Pour ~¼ cup batter per pancake.
  5. When the top begins to bubble, flip and cook until golden brown. Serve with butter and maple syrup.
