1-Hour Soft and Buttery Dinner Rolls

Breads & Rolls Bread
Serves: 15 Prep: 45 mins Cook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp active-dry yeast
  • 3½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Extra melted butter for brushing

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Grease a 9×13-inch pan.
  2. Mix water, melted butter, milk, and honey. Microwave in 15-second intervals until very warm (95–115°F) and butter is melted.
  3. Pour into stand mixer bowl, sprinkle yeast on top, stir briefly, and wait 5 minutes until foamy.
  4. Add 3½ cups flour and salt. Mix with dough hook on medium-low until combined, adding flour ¼ cup at a time if too sticky. Mix 4–5 minutes.
  5. Form dough into a ball, place in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let rise 15 minutes.
  6. Punch down and divide into 15 equal pieces. Shape into balls and place in greased pan. Cover and let rise 15–20 minutes.
  7. Bake 15 minutes or until lightly golden. Brush tops with melted butter. Serve warm.

Notes

3¾ cups flour is usually the right amount. Don't over-rise — 30 minutes total is better than a full hour. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and pan with a towel.

Banana Bread

Breads & Rolls Bread
Cook: 55 mins (large) / 33–40 mins (small) Temp: 350°F

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup Crisco
  • 3½ cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 6 bananas, mashed well

Directions

  1. Cream together sugar, eggs, and Crisco.
  2. Add flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder. Mix well.
  3. Add mashed bananas and mix until combined.
  4. Grease and flour 2 large loaf tins or 6 small tins. Fill ⅔ full.
  5. Bake at 350°F — 55 minutes for large loaves, 33–40 minutes for small loaves.

Notes

To halve the recipe, use 2 egg whites instead of a whole egg.

Bread Machine Bread – Easy As Can Be

Breads & Rolls
Yield: 1 loaf Prep: 5 mins Cook: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (227g) lukewarm water
  • ⅓ cup (74g) lukewarm milk
  • 3 tbsp (43g) butter
  • 3¾ cups (447g) King Arthur All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 tbsp (35g) sugar
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1½ tsp active dry or instant yeast

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to your bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
  2. Program for basic white bread and press Start.
  3. When done, remove the pan. After 5 minutes, shake to dislodge the loaf and turn out onto a rack to cool.
  4. Store wrapped on the counter for 4 days or freeze up to 3 months.

Copycat Texas Roadhouse Rolls

Breads & Rolls Bread
Serves: 24 rolls Prep: 2 hours Cook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 4 tbsp melted butter, separated
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups flour

Directions

  1. Bring milk to a boil, then remove from heat and let cool to lukewarm.
  2. Mix milk, yeast, and honey in a small bowl. Let sit 5 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix 3 tbsp butter, the milk mixture, egg, and 2 cups flour until smooth. Gradually add remaining 2 cups flour until a dough forms.
  4. Add salt; mix/knead 8 minutes, then knead a few more minutes on a floured surface.
  5. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise 1 hour in a warm place.
  6. Punch down dough. Roll out to ½-inch thick, fold in half, and gently seal. Cut into 24 squares.
  7. Place on greased cookie sheets, cover, and let rise 40 minutes until doubled.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 12–15 minutes until lightly golden. Brush tops with remaining 1 tbsp butter.

French Crepe Recipe

Breads & Rolls Breakfast Brunch
Serves: 8 crepes Prep: 10 mins Cook: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cup white wheat flour
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 tsp white sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, eggs, salt, vanilla, melted butter, and sugar.
  2. Gradually beat in milk with a whisk or electric mixer until smooth with no lumps.
  3. Heat a lightly buttered crepe pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour batter onto pan; tip and rotate to spread as thinly as possible.
  5. Cook 1–2 minutes per side until lightly browned. Serve hot with your favorite fillings.

Pumpkin and Sunflower Seed Bread

Breads & Rolls Bread
Yield: 2 loaves Prep: 30 mins Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 4½ tsp yeast (2 packages)
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2½–3 cups white whole wheat flour
  • ½ cup raw pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ cup raw sunflower seeds
  • Topping: 1 egg white + 1 tbsp water, 2 tbsp each pumpkin & sunflower seeds

Directions

  1. Combine water, yeast, and ¼ cup flour. Let sit 10 minutes until bubbly.
  2. Add oil, honey, salt, and 2 cups all-purpose flour. Combine.
  3. Add seeds, then add white wheat flour ¼ cup at a time until dough forms a ball and isn't too sticky.
  4. Knead in mixer for 10 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise until doubled.
  5. Punch down, divide into 2 pieces, shape into loaves, and place in greased loaf pans. Let rise 30 minutes.
  6. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with seeds. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
  7. Cool 10 minutes in pans, then transfer to a rack to cool completely.

Quick and Easy Pizza Crust

Breads & Rolls Main Dish
Yield: 1 (12-inch) crust

Ingredients

  • 1 packet (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 2½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; let stand 10 minutes until creamy.
  2. Stir in flour, salt, and oil. Beat until smooth. Let rest 10 minutes.
  3. Roll dough out on a floured surface and transfer to a lightly greased pizza pan or cornmeal-dusted peel.
  4. Add desired toppings and bake 15–20 minutes until golden brown. Cool 5 minutes before serving.

Sheet Pan Garlic Breadsticks

Breads & Rolls Bread
Serves: 18 Prep: 20 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3½–4 cups flour
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¾ cup fresh shredded Parmesan
  • Garlic powder, dried oregano, dried thyme to taste

Directions

  1. In a stand mixer with dough hook, combine water, sugar, and yeast. Rest 5 minutes.
  2. Add salt and flour; mix until incorporated. Mix on high 5 minutes, then rest 5 more minutes.
  3. Melt butter; pour half into a large baking sheet, spreading to coat the pan.
  4. Place dough in center of pan, let sit a few minutes, then spread to edges by hand.
  5. Pour remaining butter on top and spread evenly. Sprinkle with seasonings and Parmesan.
  6. Using a pizza cutter, score into breadsticks before baking.
  7. Bake at 170°F for 8 minutes to rise. Increase heat to 350°F (leaving breadsticks in) and bake 12–15 more minutes until golden.
  8. Cool a few minutes, then cut along scored lines.

The Best Cinnamon Rolls You'll Ever Eat

Breads & Rolls Breakfast Dessert
Serves: 9 large rolls Prep: 2 hours Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • Dough:
  • ¾ cup warm whole milk (110°F)
  • 2¼ tsp quick-rise or active yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, room temperature
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • Filling:
  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • Cream Cheese Frosting:
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tbsp butter, softened
  • ¾ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Warm milk to 110°F (~45 seconds in microwave). Add to mixer bowl with yeast, sugar, eggs, and melted butter. Mix well. Stir in flour and salt until dough forms.
  2. Knead with dough hook on medium for 8 minutes until smooth and slightly sticky.
  3. Place in a well-oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and a warm towel. Rise 1–1½ hours until doubled.
  4. Roll dough into a 14×9 inch rectangle. Spread softened butter, leaving a ¼-inch margin at the far edge.
  5. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle and rub into the butter.
  6. Tightly roll up from the 9-inch side. Trim ends; cut into 9 pieces with a serrated knife or floss.
  7. Place in a greased, parchment-lined 9×9 inch pan. Cover and rise 30–45 minutes.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 minutes until just slightly golden. Cool 5–10 minutes before frosting.
  9. Beat frosting ingredients until smooth and fluffy. Spread over warm rolls.

The BEST Homemade Cinnamon Rolls

Breads & Rolls Dessert
Serves: 15 Prep: 5 hours Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup milk, lukewarm
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Filling: ¼ cup butter (softened), ¾ cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • Frosting: 4 oz cream cheese, 1½ cups powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, ¼ cup melted butter, pinch salt, 2 tbsp milk

Directions

  1. Mix yeast, sugar, eggs, milk, butter, and salt together. Add 4 cups flour and mix well. Cover with plastic wrap; rise 4–6 hours on counter or overnight in the refrigerator. Do not knead.
  2. Roll out into a long, thin rectangle on a lightly floured surface. Spread softened butter.
  3. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon; spread evenly over dough. Roll tightly into a jellyroll from the long end. Cut into 1-inch pieces with string or floss.
  4. Place in a greased 9×13-inch pan. Cover and rise 30 minutes.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 20–25 minutes until golden brown. Mix and spread frosting over warm rolls.

The Best Homemade Dinner Rolls Ever!

Breads & Rolls Bread
Serves: 24 rolls Prep: 3 hours Cook: 12 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm milk (105–110°F)
  • 2 tbsp instant dry yeast
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 6 tbsp salted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (for brushing)

Directions

  1. In a stand mixer with dough hook, combine warm milk, yeast, sugar, salt, softened butter, eggs, and 5½ cups flour.
  2. Mix on low then increase to medium. Slowly add remaining ½ cup flour until dough pulls from sides (slightly sticky and soft).
  3. Transfer to a greased bowl, cover, and rise until doubled — about 90 minutes.
  4. Punch down and shape into 24 rolls. Place on a greased baking sheet in 6 rows of 4. Cover and rise until doubled, about 1 hour.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 12–14 minutes until lightly browned. Brush with melted butter. Rest 15 minutes before transferring to a rack.

Make-Ahead Tip

After the initial 90-minute rise, shape rolls, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate up to 24 hours. When ready to bake, let rolls rise at room temperature ~30 minutes while oven preheats, then bake as directed.
